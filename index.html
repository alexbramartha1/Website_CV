Majche Coffee Shop

The concept from this logo is Butterfly + Majestic Wand. The name Majche means Majestic + Psyche (Psyche means Butterfly in Greek). I used Butterfly shape to represent the name, Butterfly mean the Beautiful and Freedom of the soul when you feel the pleasure of coffee at Majche. Majestic Wand shape represent the Magical Power that makes customers feel at home in Majche for a long time.

Maouam Law Firm

Justice is a key that everyone has, this is what Maouam emphasized. Law firm that protect the key in the keyhole, means protect the justice that everyone has. The concept from this logo is Antique Keyhole + Key + Letter M. Maouam comes from the word “Maou” which means Power. They use this name to uphold justice. The colors gold and brown were chosen to represent how precious justice is.
